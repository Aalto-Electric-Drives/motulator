motulator.model.converter

Power converter models.

An inverter with constant DC-bus voltage and a frequency converter with a diode front-end rectifier are modeled. Complex space vectors are used also for duty ratios and switching states, wherever applicable. In this module, all space vectors are in stationary coordinates. The default values correspond to a 2.2-kW 400-V motor drive.
