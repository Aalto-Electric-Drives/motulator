motulator.model._converter#

Power converter models.

An inverter with constant DC-bus voltage and a frequency converter with a diode front-end rectifier are modeled. Complex space vectors are used also for duty ratios and switching states, wherever applicable. In this module, all space vectors are in stationary coordinates.

Bases: Inverter

Frequency converter.

This extends the Inverter class with models for a strong grid, a three-phase diode-bridge rectifier, an LC filter, and a three-phase inverter.

Parameters:

  • L (float) – DC-bus inductance (H).

  • C (float) – DC-bus capacitance (F).

  • U_g (float) – Grid voltage (V, line-line, rms).

  • f_g (float) – Grid frequency (Hz).
